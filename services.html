What We Build

AI Automation Services for Your Business

Every engagement starts with understanding your operations — what's working, what's not, and where AI can make the biggest impact. We don't sell packages. We solve problems.

1. Lead Capture & Follow-Up Automation

The problem

Leads come in from forms, emails, and enquiries — but follow-up is slow, inconsistent, or entirely manual. Opportunities go cold before anyone responds.

What we build

  • AI-powered lead qualification from forms, emails, and chat
  • Automated, personalized follow-up sequences
  • CRM updates and task creation so nothing falls through

Who this helps: Any business where inbound leads need fast, consistent follow-up — agencies, professional services, trades, e-commerce, SaaS.

2. Customer Support & Helpdesk Automation

The problem

Support volume is growing, response times are slipping, and the team is spending too much time on repetitive questions that have clear answers.

What we build

  • AI agents that resolve standard queries using your knowledge base
  • Intelligent ticket triage and routing to the right person
  • Auto-drafted responses for your team to review and send

Who this helps: Businesses with growing support volume — SaaS, e-commerce, service companies, agencies managing client communications.

3. Internal Operations & Task Routing

The problem

Communication is scattered across email, Slack, Teams, and everything in between. Tasks get lost. Coordination is manual. Nobody has a clear picture of what's happening.

What we build

  • Turn messages and emails into structured, routed tasks
  • Auto-assign work to the right team or queue
  • Daily and weekly summaries of projects, decisions, and progress

Who this helps: Any team where coordination overhead is eating into productive time — operations teams, agencies, growing businesses with multiple moving parts.

4. Document & Data Processing Automation

The problem

Staff spend hours on manual data entry — copying information between systems, processing invoices, handling forms and contracts by hand.

What we build

  • AI-powered extraction from PDFs, emails, invoices, and forms
  • Validation and enrichment of records
  • Automatic syncing to your CRM, accounting, or project management tools

Who this helps: Businesses processing high volumes of documents — finance teams, legal, operations, any company dealing with paperwork bottlenecks.
