The Process

How We Work With You

Every engagement follows the same structured process — from understanding your operations to delivering a working solution. No guesswork. No generic templates. Built around your business.

01

Discovery & Operational Audit

What we do:

We learn how your business operates day-to-day. We map your workflows, identify where time is being lost, and pinpoint the specific bottlenecks where AI and automation will make the biggest difference.

What you do:

Walk us through your operations honestly — what works, what doesn't, and where the friction is. The more we understand, the better the solution.

What you get:

A clear picture of your operational landscape and a prioritized list of opportunities — ranked by impact and feasibility.

02

Solution Design & Prototyping

What we do:

Based on the audit, we design the workflow — what gets automated, what AI handles, how it connects to your existing tools, and what the end result looks like. We prototype before we build so you see the solution taking shape early.

What you do:

Review the proposed design, give feedback, and confirm it matches how your team actually works. This is where we align before any code is written.

What you get:

A concrete solution blueprint — clear scope, defined integrations, and a shared understanding of what's being built and why.

03

Build & Integration

What we do:

We build the solution and integrate it with your existing systems — CRMs, communication tools, project management platforms, cloud services, whatever your business runs on. Everything is built to fit, not to force a change.

What you do:

Provide access to the systems we'll integrate with and stay available for quick questions as we build. We keep you informed throughout.

What you get:

A working AI workflow or automation, integrated into your operations and ready for rollout.

04

Implementation, Training & Support

What we do:

We roll out the solution with your team, provide hands-on training so everyone knows how to use it, and stay close during the initial period to catch and resolve any issues quickly.

What you do:

Use it. Give us feedback on what's working and what needs adjustment. The first few weeks are about refining.

What you get:

A fully operational system your team actually uses — not a demo that sits on a shelf. Plus ongoing support to keep it running and evolving as your business grows.

Typical Timeline

Most initial builds launch within 4–6 weeks depending on scope and complexity.

Simpler automations can be live in days. We'll give you a realistic timeline during the Discovery phase.

What You Need in Place

Before we start, it helps to have:

  • ✓ Willingness to walk us through your current operations openly
  • ✓ Access to the systems and tools we'll be integrating with
  • ✓ A point of contact who can make decisions and give feedback
  • ✓ An understanding that the best solutions come from iteration — not perfection on day one
